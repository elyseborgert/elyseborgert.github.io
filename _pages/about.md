Welcome to my website! 

I recently joined [JMP Statistical Discovery](https://www.jmp.com/en/home) in Cary, NC as a Research Statistician Developer. Previously, I held postdoctoral positions at the [University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill Thurston Arthritis Research Center](https://www.med.unc.edu/tarc/) and the [U.S. Meat Animal Research Center](https://www.ars.usda.gov/plains-area/clay-center-ne/marc/). I earned my PhD in [Statistics and Operations Research at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill](https://stor.unc.edu/) under the joint supervision of [Jan Hannig](https://hannig.cloudapps.unc.edu/) and [J.S. Marron](https://marron.web.unc.edu/). 

If you are interested in my research or want to connect, please feel free to send me an email. 

## Research Interests
- Foundations of statistics
- Machine learning for complex and high-dimensional data objects
- Functional data analysis
- Mixed effects models
- Applications of statistics in medicine and human movement science
- Applications of statistics in agriculture and animal breeding
